Age 85: Age of Intuition

UTOPIA

It is an age of wartime and hostility where several kingdoms exist, with each kingdom made up of several individual provinces working together as a team. Each kingdom can hold up to 25 different provinces. Each province is run by a different individual which has the ability to create the province's and ruler's name. The individual can possibly get the luxury of picking from a variety of races and personalities to play. Depending on the kingdom where that individual plays in, the kingdom's setup (race and personality) is determined by the monarch and stewards of that kingdom. The goal is to be the best kingdom in Utopia. There are goals that can be accomplished by provinces and by kingdoms. Winning the war crown, having highest networth, highest honor, and highest land rank. Each age can last up to three months usually. Come and join the thousands of players today play Utopia- A world of intrigue, strategy, and warfare. While at it, try your luck at being the best...
